# Local VS Cloud

| Feature | Local Hosting | Cloud Hosting |
|---|---|---|
| **Cost** | High initial investment for hardware, but no ongoing subscription fees. | Lower initial cost, but with ongoing subscription fees based on usage. |
| **Performance** | Limited by the power of local hardware. Can be very fast with high-end hardware, but may not be able to handle large models. | Can be scaled to meet the needs of the model. Can be very fast, but can also be slow if the model is not optimized for the cloud. |
| **Scalability** | Difficult to scale. Requires purchasing new hardware. | Easy to scale. Can be scaled up or down as needed. |
| **Privacy** | High privacy. Data is not sent to a third party. | Lower privacy. Data is sent to a third party, which may have access to it. |
| **Control** | Full control over the model and its environment. | Limited control over the model and its environment. |
| **Maintenance** | Requires ongoing maintenance of hardware and software. | No maintenance required. The cloud provider handles all maintenance. |
| **Accessibility** | Can only be accessed from the local network. | Can be accessed from anywhere with an internet connection. |
